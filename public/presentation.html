The Problem

Why Teams Struggle with Scrum

🧩
Fragmented Tools

Jira, Trello, Notion, and spreadsheets — all separate, never in sync. Teams waste hours context-switching between platforms and hunting for the latest information.

📉
No Real Visibility

Can't see velocity, burndown, or team health in one place. Managers fly blind, retrospectives lack data, and sprint forecasts are little more than guesses.

🐌
Manual Everything

Standups, retrospectives, and risk tracking done ad hoc — in Slack, docs, or verbal agreements. Nothing is tracked, nothing is actioned, and nothing improves.

Key Features

Built for Real Scrum Teams

⚡
Real-time Kanban Board

Drag & drop, WIP limits, swimlanes by assignee, epic, or priority. Updates sync instantly across all team members.

📈
Velocity & Burndown

Auto-calculated charts with sprint predictions and cumulative flow diagram analysis. Know your team's true capacity.

🗺️
Visual Roadmap

Gantt-style epic, sprint, and milestone timeline. Visualize dependencies and communicate progress to stakeholders instantly.

🤖
AI Assistant

Smart sprint planning suggestions and automated risk detection. Surface blockers before they derail your sprint.

📋
Story Management

Subtasks, attachments, acceptance criteria, and full dev workflow. Every story has complete context — no more Slack archaeology.

👥
Team Workload

Per-member capacity bars and overload alerts. Balance the work before you plan the sprint, not after the damage is done.
